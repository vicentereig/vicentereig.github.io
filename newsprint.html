Tired of Prompting the Model, He Programmed It

DSPy.rb is Stanford’s DSPy in Ruby — signatures, modules, and optimizers in place of brittle prompt strings. Turns out other people were tired of prompting too.

The Command-Line Desk

Tools that give people direct access to their own data — no dashboards, no waiting, just a terminal and a question. Most began as something the maintainer needed himself.

  • quaid Get your chats back — export ChatGPT and Claude conversations locally. →
  • plausible-cli Plausible Analytics for AI agents and humans. →
  • garmin-cli Pull your Garmin Connect data from the terminal. →
  • whatsapp-cli WhatsApp from your terminal, with a JSON interface for automation. →
  • orb Orb billing forensics from the terminal. →
  • lf-cli Query Langfuse traces and metrics from the terminal. →
  • exa-ruby Typed Ruby client and CLI for the Exa.ai search API. →
